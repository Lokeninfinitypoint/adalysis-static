Campaign Studio

Brief, build, and launch paid media campaigns alongside the teammates who execute and approve every change.

  1. Products
  2. Campaign Studio

Campaign Studio centralizes all the details that make or break a launch—budget guardrails, naming conventions, experiment plans, and team assignments. Every step is logged, so stakeholders can audit decisions without slowing work down.

  • Reusable launch briefs. Standardize objectives, target audiences, and success indicators with templates your team trusts.
  • Cross-network builds. Activate campaigns for Google Ads and Microsoft Advertising from one guided workflow.
  • Guardrails & approvals. Require two-person reviews or automated checks before budgets go live.
  • Asset library. Store headlines, visuals, and offers that are ready for the next promotion.

How teams use Campaign Studio

  1. Start from a shared brief that aligns marketing, finance, and leadership on goals.
  2. Import recommended keywords, audiences, and budgets directly into draft campaigns.
  3. Assign tasks to copywriters, designers, and approvers so everyone knows their role.
  4. Launch with automated checks that catch naming, URL, or budget inconsistencies.
