Onboarding · Set up your workspace

Set up user accounts, control permissions, and enable Microsoft sign-in to keep everyone productive from day one.

Add all account users

  1. Go to Settings > Users in the left navigation.
  2. Select Add user and enter the teammate’s name and email address.
  3. Choose the workspaces, accounts, or client groups they should access.
  4. Click Send invite. The teammate receives an email to finish their registration.

Tip: Send invites before your go-live date so users can confirm access and bookmark the login link ahead of time.

Important: Fewer pending invites means fewer approval bottlenecks when you launch new campaigns or schedule automations.

Make someone’s access read or edit

  1. Open the user’s record from the Settings > Users list.
  2. In the Permissions tab, choose Read-only or Can edit for each workspace.
  3. If you need granular control, limit edit access to specific accounts and keep others read-only.
  4. Save your changes. They take effect immediately for that teammate.

Note: Analysts who only need to pull reports can stay read-only, while campaign owners should have edit rights to manage bids and budgets.

Let users access Waduit with Microsoft

  1. From Settings > Authentication, enable Microsoft single sign-on.
  2. Copy the Login with Microsoft link and share it with your team in the invite email or chat thread.
  3. Ask teammates to choose Continue with Microsoft the first time they sign in and authorize their Azure AD account.
  4. Once connected, they can bypass passwords and use their Microsoft credentials for every future login.

Reminder: Microsoft SSO keeps access consistent with your company’s identity policies and simplifies offboarding later.

Frequently asked questions

Can I resend an invite to someone who missed it?

Yes. Find the user in Settings > Users, open the menu next to their name, and select Resend invite. The email arrives instantly with a fresh activation link.

How do I know who still needs to finish registration?

Pending users are labeled Awaiting acceptance in the user list. Filter by status to follow up with anyone who hasn’t joined yet.

What happens when someone leaves the company?

Open their user profile, revoke access to all workspaces, and select Deactivate. This removes them from future notifications and frees the seat for a new teammate.
